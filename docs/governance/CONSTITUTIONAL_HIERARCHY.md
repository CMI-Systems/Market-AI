# Constitutional Hierarchy — Market-AI / AICC

Market-AI and AICC operate beneath the CMI-Systems Constitution. Market-AI
computes and serves operational market intelligence. AICC presents approved
intelligence to the operator. Neither domain owns canonical database
infrastructure, dataset certification, training activation, autonomous
learning, or constitutional amendment.

Uncertain authority fails closed. Production, migrations, learning, autonomous
execution, and protected-branch merge require separate explicit approval.
